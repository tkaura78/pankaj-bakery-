Our Story

Founded with a passion for baking and community, Pankaj Bakery began as a small family-run shop in Basti Guzan Main Bazar. Today, we continue our tradition of crafting fresh breads, cakes, and snacks with quality ingredients and a whole lot of love.

Our mission is simple: to bring joy through every bite while upholding values of freshness, honesty, and hospitality.
